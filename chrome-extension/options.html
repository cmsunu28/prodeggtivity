prodEggtivity Options

Set the URLs you use for work under "Productive URLs" and the ones you use for play under "Unproductive URLs." Put each URL on its own line.

Any subsets of the URLs will also be included in the category you choose. For example, if you type "reddit.com" under Unproductive URLs, everything that begins with "reddit.com" (reddit.com/r/all, reddit.com/r/funny) will stop your chickens from running.
